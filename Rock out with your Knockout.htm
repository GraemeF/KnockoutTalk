What is KnockoutJS?

  • MVVM for your web browser
  • Bind UI elements to your models

What's MVVM?

  • Model – View – ViewModel
    • Model: Your domain object
    • View: DOM elements that present your model
    • ViewModel: Maps between Model and View using binding

Why care?

  • Separation of concerns
  • Testability
  • Events? What events?
  • Familiarity, at least for me!
